Must-Know Mastery Checklist

What to Memorize (vs Understand vs Practice)

Memorize:
  • Primal→Dual conversion table
  • Newsvendor critical fractile formula
  • Triangular distribution CDF and inverse (likely provided but be safe)
  • Flow conservation equation
  • TSP degree + SEC formulas
  • Bid-price decision rule
  • Side-constraint patterns (if A then B; at most K; etc.)
Understand (don't memorize):
  • WHY degree constraints alone fail in TSP (subtours)
  • WHY log-transform converts max-reliability to shortest path
  • WHY customized bound > achievable revenue (heterogeneous segments)
  • WHY LP relaxation gives upper bound (larger feasible set)
  • WHY strong constraints give faster solves (tighter LP bound)
Practice (until automatic):
  • Write LP formulations from word problems (variables → objective → constraints)
  • Trace Python code by hand (getSubtours, regression tree leaf lookup)
  • Integrate piecewise densities for expected profit
  • Identify problem type from text (TSP vs shortest path vs MCNF vs p-median)
